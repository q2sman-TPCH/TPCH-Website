Membership

Join TPCH and become part of Tauranga's Bengali and Eastern Indian community — celebrating culture, preserving heritage and building belonging together.

Why join TPCH

More than a membership

Being a TPCH member means being part of a community that truly understands what it means to carry two homes in your heart. It means your children grow up knowing where they come from. It means belonging — in the fullest sense of the word.

Access to all events

Priority access to the Probasi Cultural Festival, Durga Puja, Rabindra Jayanti and all community gatherings throughout the year.

Community meals

Members enjoy catered meals at Durga Puja and other community occasions — the flavours of home, shared together.

Cultural programmes

Participation in Bengali language activities, music and dance workshops, children's programmes and cultural camps.

Children included free

Children of member families participate in all TPCH activities at no additional cost — investing in the next generation.

Community network

Connect with Bengali and Eastern Indian families across Tauranga and the Bay of Plenty — a ready-made community from day one.

Have your say

Members have voting rights at TPCH's Annual General Meeting, shaping the direction of the organisation.

Questions & answers

Frequently asked questions

When does membership run from?

TPCH membership runs annually from 1 April to 31 March. If you join mid-year, your membership covers the remainder of that membership year.

Do I need to be Bengali to join?

Not at all. TPCH welcomes anyone with a connection to or love of Bengali and Eastern Indian culture — families, friends and those simply curious about our community.

How many children can I include?

All children in a member household are included free of charge — there is no limit on the number of children.

Can I attend events as a non-member?

Many of our events are open to all — including the Probasi Cultural Festival and the post-festival community meal. Some activities and meals are reserved for members. Get in touch for details.

How do I renew my membership?

We will be in touch ahead of renewal each year. Simply make another bank transfer and let us know — renewal takes just a few minutes.

What if I have more questions?

Please email us at tpchconnect@gmail.com — we are happy to answer any questions about membership or our community.
